Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. Introduction

At Sigl Financial LLC (“Sigl Financial,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, or details you provide when contacting us.
  • Business Information: Financial or organizational data shared for bookkeeping, payroll, or advisory purposes.
  • Technical Information: Basic analytics such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected through standard web tools (e.g., server logs, Google Search Console).

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide agreed-upon services.
  • Maintain accurate records for client engagements.
  • Improve our website, communications, and client experience.
  • Comply with applicable professional and legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud accounting platforms, email providers, or file storage systems) strictly as needed to deliver our services. All providers are expected to maintain appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain client and business records for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Non-client inquiries may be deleted sooner at our discretion.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at michael@siglfinancial.com. We will respond in a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that information from a child under 13 has been provided, we will delete it promptly.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
